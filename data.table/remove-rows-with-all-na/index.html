Remove rows with all NA

Scott Prevost

2019/02/28

Remove rows with all NA but keep any row with at least 1 observation:

dt <- dt[rowSums(is.na(x)) <= 1]

dt <- dt %>%
  .[rowSums(is.na(.)) <= 1]
