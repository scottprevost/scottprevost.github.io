Add subscripts and superscripts to ggplot axis titles

Scott Prevost

2019/03/11

Temperature superscript

bquote("Temperature (" * "" ^o * "F)")

Natural gas superscript

bquote("Natural gas (" * "m" ^3 * ")")

Carbon dioxide subscript

bquote("CO" * "" [2])
